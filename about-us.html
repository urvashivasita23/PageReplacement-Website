About Us

Who we are

We are Information and Communication Technology undergraduates in Pandit Deendayal Petroleum University, Gandhinagar, India. We undertook this project as a part of Operating Systems Lab under Mr. Chintan Patel.

We learnt a great deal about Web-Development, Page Replacement Algorithms, Operating Systems, Team management and coordination through this project. We really hope that you had a great learning experience from our website.

About the website

Welcome to our Website
